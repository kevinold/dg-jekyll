DICOM Grid CEO – Mike Wall – to Sit on iHT2 Health IT Summit Panel

Cloud computing imaging expert brings unique clinical data exchange perspective to April 5 HIE discussion

WHAT:                       
Mike Wall, CEO of DICOM Grid, will join a panel of healthcare IT executives at the Institute for Health Technology Transformation (iHT2) Health IT Summit in Atlanta, April 5-6, 2011, to discuss Health Information Exchanges and solutions to overcome the challenges and objections from various stakeholders for implementing them.

The entire iHT2 Health IT Summit agenda is available at http://ihealthtran.com/atlantaagenda.html. iHT2 is the leading organization committed to bringing together private and public sector leaders fostering the growth and meaningful use of technology across the healthcare industry.

PANEL DETAILS:           
"Intelligent Health Information Exchange (HIE): Utilizing analytics with HIE data to provide proactive care"

The benefits of healthcare information technology won’t be realized until all stakeholders truly participate in HIEs. The panel will present examples and results of successful data exchange and address how true HIE can be achieved by:

  • Engaging all stakeholder in the process of defining, developing and using HIE services
  • Ensuring trust and support from all stakeholders
  • Ensuring the privacy, security and quality of data

The panel will be moderated by Scot Peterson, Editorial Director for CIO/IT Strategy Media Group, and will include:

  • Mike Wall, CEO of DICOM Grid
  • Scott Afzal, HIE Program Director of Chesapeake Regional Information System for our Patients
  • Dan Porreca, Executive Director of HEALTHeLINK
  • Art Glasgow, Chief Technology Officer of Ingenix
  • Thomas Cooke, VP of Business Development Axolotl Corporation                

CONFERENCE ATTENDEES:            
Attendees include CEOs, CIOs, chief medical information officers, chief medical informatics officers, physicians, practice managers, and vice presidents/directors of IT.
