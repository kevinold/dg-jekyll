DICOM GRID CHOSEN TO PRESENT AMONG ELITE AT HIMSS HEALTHCARE TECHNOLOGY SUMMIT

PHOENIX – Nov.  20, 2012 – DICOM Grid announced today that they have been selected to present at the annual mHealth Summit-HIMSS Mobile Venture Fair. Hailed as the strategic partner’s front row seat to cutting edge mobile healthcare technology product innovations, the HIMSS Mobile Venture Fair is considered a prime networking opportunity for Health IT investors.

The HIMSS panel of industry experts provides attendees with the best investment opportunities from emerging and established mobile IT companies by pre-qualifying presenters. DICOM Grid was chosen to present from an elite group of candidates who applied from all over America, Canada, and Argentina.

"Mobile technology is transforming health care delivery," says Morris Panner, CEO of DICOM Grid "We are looking forward to speaking about the intersection of health and mobile information technology. Our company is honored and grateful for the opportunity to collaborate with health care stakeholders on how mobile technology reduces healthcare costs, improves health outcomes, access and profitability."

DICOM Grid, a leader in the medical image exchange industry, offers the only proven big-data medical image management and mobility viewing solution. They will be presenting on Wednesday, December 5, 2012 at the Gaylord National Resort and Convention Center in National Harbor, MD.

Register to attend the mHealth Summit and Mobile Venture Fair at www.mhealthsummit.org.

About mHeath Summit
The mHealth Summit is presented by the HIMSS Media, in partnership with the National Institutes of Health, the mHealth Alliance and the Foundation for the NIH. It is the leading conference, expo and networking event advancing cross-sector collaboration in the best use of wireless technology to improve health outcomes. The 2012 mHealth Summit, to be held December 3-5 at the Gaylord National Resort and Convention Center, will connect leaders in government, private sector/industry, academia, foundations and not-for-profit organizations to advance discussion and decision-making related to the intersection of mobile technology, health research, and policy in the United States and abroad. For more information, visit http://www.mheathsummit.org.

About HIMSS
HIMSS is a cause-based, not-for-profit organization exclusively focused on providing global leadership for the optimal use of information technology (IT) and management systems for the betterment of healthcare. HIMSS frames and leads healthcare practices and public policy through its content expertise, professional development, research initiatives, and media vehicles designed to promote information and management systems’ contributions to improving the quality, safety, access, and cost-effectiveness of patient care. To learn more visit http://www.himss.org.
