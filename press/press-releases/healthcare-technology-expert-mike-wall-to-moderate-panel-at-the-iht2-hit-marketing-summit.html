Healthcare technology expert, Mike Wall, to Moderate Panel at the iHT2 HIT Marketing Summit

WHO:                
Mike Wall, CEO of DICOM Grid, is an experienced leader in high-technology companies and has guided numerous healthcare information technology (IT) start-up companies to success.  Before joining DICOM Grid in 2008, Mike advised new storage solution companies through his own consultant group, Wall Technology Advisors.  Mike also served as the general manager for Intel Corporation’s Storage Group, Intel’s Supercomputer Systems Division and Cray Research’s North American Operations. Currently, Mike sits on the Boards of DICOM Grid, Atempo, and Nirvanix.

WHAT:              
Wall will moderate a panel session, “Staying Ahead of the Curve:  Being prepared for the next steps for Meaningful Use and other regulations,” at the 2011 iHT2 HIT Marketing Summit. 

PANEL DETAILS:        
"Staying Ahead of the Curve:  Being prepared for the next steps for Meaningful Use and other regulations"

The healthcare IT industry is flourishing in large part because of meaningful use.  The panel will address ways for your company to benefit from meaningful use by:

  • Moving into the next phases of meaningful use and how your company can be at the forefront of the change.
  • Educating your clients and keeping them in compliance.
  • Reimbursement funds.

Panel participants will share the methods they are using today and plans for future phases:

  • Dave Tank, Director of Vertical Market Management, Level 3 Communications
  • Susan Harmon, Director of Government Affairs, Athenahealth
  • Amnon Gavish, Vice President of Vertical Solutions, Vidyo

The conference agenda is available at: http://www.ihealthtran.com/hitmsummitconfagenda.html.
