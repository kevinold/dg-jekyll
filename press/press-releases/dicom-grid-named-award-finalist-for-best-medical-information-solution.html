DICOM Grid Named Award Finalist for Best Medical Information Solution

DG Suite Earns Prestigious Information Services Industry Recognition

WASHINGTON, D.C. (December 20, 2012) DICOM Grid announced today that their medical image exchange platform was named a finalist product in the 2013 SIIA Content CODiE Awards for Best Medical Information Solution.

Serving as the pre-eminent awards program for the software and information industries, the SIIA CODiE Awards have been honoring excellence for 27 years. This yearâ€™s program has 75 categories organized by industry focus in content, education, and software.

DICOM Grid offers a cloud-based platform used for the distribution of medical imaging data. The scalable solution enables organizations to easily deploy applications for medical imaging collaboration, image data management, and vendor neutral archiving. The solution protects data using patented security technology and integrates with a variety of outputs to deliver a seamless end-user experience. In just a few clicks, patients and care providers can share complex medical images across different institutions and platforms.

"The CODiE Awards competition was extremely competitive this year with a record number of innovative products and solutions competing," said Kathy Greenler Sexton, VP & General Manager for the Content Division at SIIA. "All of our CODiE Awards finalists should be commended for their innovation and market-leading products and services."

Finalists represent the best products, technologies, and services created by information services providers. CODiE Award winners will be announced during a special luncheon on January 31st in New York City during the SIIA's annual flagship conference for information industry leaders, IIS 2013: Breakthrough.

About the SIIA

The Software & Information Industry Association (SIIA) is the principal trade association for the software and digital content industries, providing global services in government relations, business development, corporate education, and intellectual property protection to more than 700 organizations world-wide. The SIIA Content Division is one of six industry-focused divisions, and serves the media, publishing, information services and technology companies that drive the $462 billion content industry. For more information, visit http://www.siia.net/content.
