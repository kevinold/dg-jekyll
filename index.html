Secure and intuitive Medical Image Exchange for patient care teams, irrespective of location, with automated workflow. Perfect for mobile devices on low bandwidth links.

Effective, secure, and scalable solutions to managing a global clinical trial.

Cost effective, flexible, and secure retention and disaster recovery for medical images. View the archive from anywhere on any device, implement retention policies, and quick restore a single study or an entire PACS.

Import images and studies from a wide range of media (CDs, DVDs, thumb drives), and assure the imports and correct demographic data are properly imported into PACS.
